SQLite is a lightweight, standard database. Every Val Town account comes with its own private SQLite database that is accessible from any of your vals.

Val Town SQLite is backed by [Turso](https://turso.tech/).

- [Usage](./usage)
- [Migrations](./migrations)
- [ORMs](./orms)

You may find this admin viewer helpful for simple debug views of your database: https://www.val.town/v/stevekrouse/sqlite_admin

## Limits

You can store 10mb on the free plan and up to 1gb on the paid plan. Contact us if you need more space.
